We are a technology-driven engineering firm based in **Ballarpur, Maharashtra**, specializing in full-stack software development, Industrial IoT (IIoT), and AI-driven applications. We partner with businesses to transform complex operational challenges into scalable, secure, and user-centric digital solutions. 

## 🌐 Enterprise & Client Solutions

Our current portfolio encompasses private client repositories and proprietary products across high-impact sectors:

* **Connected Healthcare & Wearables:** Developing custom mobile applications and software infrastructure with seamless Bluetooth (BLE) integration for real-time medical device monitoring (e.g., Blood Pressure, SpO2).
* **Smart Healthcare Kiosks:** Engineering fully software-driven, interactive kiosk interfaces for streamlined patient data management and accessibility.
* **IoT Geo-Fencing & Smart Perimeters:** Building robust, sensor-driven perimeter security and spatial monitoring systems for agricultural and industrial environments.
* **EdTech SaaS Ecosystems:** Scaling a proprietary SaaS platform designed to foster deep, interactive connections between educational institutes and their student bodies.
